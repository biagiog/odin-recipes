Crunch wrap recipe

Ingredients

    Meat filling

  • Beef mince
  • Beef oxo cube
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cumin
  • Chilli powder
  • Oil

    • cheese sauce

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese

    • Other ingredients

  • Doritos
  • Tortilla wraps
  • Sour cream
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

    Meat filling

  1. Put beef mince in pan with oil.
  2. Crumble oxo cube and sprinkle over meat along with the other herbs and fry until cooked.

    Cheese Sauce

  1. Make a Roux with the butter and flour by heating up the butter in a sauce pan, adding flour and mixing
  2. Slowly add milk while mixing with a wisk until it has a thick sauce like consistency
  3. Add grated cheese (save a little for later) and mix until fully incorporated

    Building the wrap

  1. Cut one tortilla into a small circle to use as a lid
  2. Get a full sized tortilla and place some of the mince filling into the middle
  3. Pour some cheese sauce over the mince
  4. Put a layer of doritos on top
  5. cover doritos in soured cream
  6. add lettuce, tomatos and some grated cheese
  7. place small tortilla lid on top and fold the larger tortilla over the smaller one at all edges to make a hexagon
  8. flip over into a frying pan and fry with a little oil at a low or medium heat to seal the edges
